### 💫 About Me

I am a passionate frontend developer dedicated to crafting highly interactive, pixel-perfect web experiences. My journey into tech is built on a foundation of diverse professional experiences—ranging from military service and chemical engineering to English instruction and video wall operations—which have equipped me with exceptional problem-solving skills, discipline, and a meticulous eye for detail. I specialize in building responsive, modern user interfaces that merge beautiful design with robust performance. Whether I am architecting a complex component layout or animating a user journey, my goal is always to bring static concepts to life through smooth interactions and intuitive user flows.

- 🔭 **Currently focusing on:** Advanced UI/UX patterns and building immersive web applications.
- 🌱 **Deepening my expertise in:** React.js, Next.js, and complex GSAP animations.
- ⚡ **Goal:** Engineering high-performance, responsive interfaces that delight users.
- 🎨 **Passion:** Bridging the gap between design and engineering with clean, maintainable code.
